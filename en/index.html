A Heritage of Love and Labor

The Lagotto Romagnolo is more than just a pet; it is a centuries-old legacy of Italian craftsmanship. From the marshlands of Comacchio to the forests of Romagna, these intelligent souls have been our loyal partners in the hunt for the world's finest truffles.

A dog that thinks with its nose

The Lagotto Romagnolo was originally a water retriever from the marshes of Romagna. After the marshes were drained in the 19th century, it transformed into a truffle specialist – work it carries in its genes and in its standard to this day.

It is a highly intelligent, energetic working dog with a suppressed prey drive and a deep bond with its family.
